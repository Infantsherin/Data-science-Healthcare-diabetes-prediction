About DiabetesPredict

How It Works

DiabetesPredict uses a machine learning model trained on historical medical data to assess your risk of diabetes based on key health indicators. Our algorithm analyzes patterns in your input data to generate a probability score.

The Model

The predictive model was developed using Python's scikit-learn library, trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset. It achieves an accuracy of over 85% in clinical validation tests.
